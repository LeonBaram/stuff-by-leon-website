A Little Bit About Me

I'm a big believer in function over form. I have a passion for creating beautiful, minimalist products that place the needs of the user first. To me, that means making my applications responsive, accessible, and performant, using the best modern technologies that fit the job.

My time in the food service industry has acclimated me to fast-paced and customer-focused work environments. It also taught me the value of teamwork, and greatly contributed to my confidence in communicating, collaborating, and problem-solving with others.

I will never stop learning, and I will never stop helping others learn. The best way to understand something is to try to explain it to someone else; this has always been my learning style. I am not afraid to ask for help, and I never turn down anyone who asks for it. I firmly believe that lifting each other up is the truest path to success.
